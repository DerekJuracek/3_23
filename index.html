Welcome to TROVE!

Click the button below to explore voter turnout in Nebraska! Voter Turnout represents the total number of ballots cast out of the total number of registered voters for 2020, 2022 and 2024 Elections. Type in an address, ZIP code, city, or county in the search bar and then zoom in to view a specific area. When you click or tap within your chosen area, you will see a pop-up appear with more information. Voter turnout data is from the Nebraska Secretary of State's records and is available at the Census tract, ZIP code, and County level (under the 'Select Data' menu). Demographic data are available at the Census tract level and are taken from the Census Bureau's American Community Survey 2021 5-year estimates.
